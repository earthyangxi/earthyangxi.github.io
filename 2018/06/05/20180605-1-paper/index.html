Examining CNN Representations With Respect To Dataset Bias

Posted on 2018-06-05 | In Paper

diagnose feature representations of the CNN.

Introduction

representations flaws caused by potential bias in the collection of training samples.
ref: Unbiased look at dataset, 2011

CNN representations may be biased even when the CNN achieves a high accuracy on testing samples, because testing samples may have a similar bias.

compute the relationship between each pair of attributes (Ai, Aj), i.e. identifying whether Ai is positively/negatively/not related to Aj.

attributes always co-appear

Method:

mine relationships
annotate some ground-truth attribute relationships
compute the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between the mined relationships and ground-truth relationships to discover attributes that are not well learned.

In fact, how to define ground-truth relationships is still an open problem.
